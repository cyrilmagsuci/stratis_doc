Support¶

If you’re having issues with any part of the process or want to provide us with any feedback, join us in the #sidechains channel on Discord.

Report a Bug¶

You can also raise any issues you have along the way in the StratisBitcoinFullNode GitHub repository. Please apply the sidechains label to any issues that are specific to sidechains.

For anything urgent, feel free to contact us in the #sidechains channel in Discord.
